About the Book

Some love stories are built to survive the fall. Claire thought her life was complete—a devoted husband, two beautiful children, a home built on promises whispered in the dark. But when a trip back to her hometown unearths old ghosts and aching loneliness, Claire makes one terrible mistake that shatters the world she fought so hard to protect. Now, faced with the wreckage of trust and the weight of her own choices, Claire must find the courage to tell the truth—and risk losing everything she once believed unbreakable. In the aftermath of betrayal, Claire and Ethan stand at a crossroads: Will love be enough to heal what was broken? Or are some wounds too deep to survive?

Every Day, Always is a soul-stirring novel about the fragile beauty of forgiveness, the brutal honesty real love demands, and the breathtaking power of choosing each other—not just once, but over and over again. Raw, heartbreaking, and unforgettable, Elena Hart’s debut will leave readers aching, hoping, and believing in the extraordinary courage it takes to stay.
